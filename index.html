G'Day! I'm Lex.

Who Am I?

I'm a middle aged Mum from the little island state off the southeast coast of Australia. I live with my 92yo grandmother, my 15yo son and my two Birman cats.

I love learning about almost everything but have a particular interest in maths and technology. I've wanted to learn frontend development for some time, so enrolling in this Techdegree seems like a logical step towards that...
Besides if I don't bite the bullet and get started, there's a very real chance I may start turning into a 'crazy cat lady' and end up living out my days alone with my 28 cats!

What else?
I became a tennis umpire at age 17 and have worked at the Australian Open and other professional events most years since. I've umpired all the greats of the game (and some of the not so greats) over the last 20 years! Brag moment!

I've studied Mathematics, Economics, Sport Management, Human Resources, Education, Media, Writing for the Web, Philosophy, Psychology and French at university level. But I find the best learning is done informally on my own, and there are so many cool and interesting things out there to be learned.

This profile page marks the start of my learning journey in frontend development.
